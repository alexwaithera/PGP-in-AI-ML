Thera Bank Personal Loan Campaign¶

The dataset contains data on 5000 customers. The data include customer demographic information (age, income, etc.), the customer's relationship with the bank (mortgage, securities account, etc.), and the customer response to the last personal loan campaign (Personal Loan). Among these 5000 customers, only 480 (= 9.6%) accepted the personal loan that was offered to them in the earlier campaign.

Domain: Banking

Context:

This case is about a bank (TheraBank) whose management wants to explore ways of converting its liability customers to personal loan customers (while retaining them as depositors). A campaign that the bank ran last year for liability customers showed a healthy conversion rate of over 9% success. This has encouraged the retail marketing department to devise campaigns with better target marketing to increase the success ratio with a minimal budget.

Attribute Information: Customer ID; Age; Professional Experience; CCAVg (Average spending on credit cards); Mortgage; Personal Loan; SecuritiesAccount with the bank; CDA Account with the bank; Online(Internet Banking Facilities); CreditCard issued by the bank; Zip Code; Family size; Income.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Exploratory Data Analysis
  • Preparing the data to train amodel
  • Training and making predictions using a classification model
  • Model evaluation

Objective: The classification goal is to predict the likelihood of a liability customer buying personal loans.

Observations:

  • Running different solvers i.e. 'newton-cg', 'lbfgs', 'liblinear', 'sag', 'saga' build a solver with a training accuracy of 0.947 and testing accuracy of 0.956 for all the solvers.
  • The only solver parameter that seem to have a higher testing accuracy compared with the training accuracy is 'saga' and hence was selected. The 'saga' solver works well with l1 regularization. l2 regularization reduced the testing accuracy compared to the training accuracy. It is good to have a high testing than traning accuracy.
  • The 'C' value that seem to have a high accuracy is 0.25 at 0.869 training accuracy with a 0.876
  • This model has resulted in increasing the Recall metric from the initial 0.64 to 0.97 as well as increasing the the ROC AUC Curve from 0.81 to 0.92. Although changing the parameters has also reduced the F1 score and precision significantly. The F1 score has reduced from 0.74 to 0.59 while Precision has reduced from 0.88 to 0.42.
  • Overall, the model accuracy has reduced from the initial 0.96 (for both training and testing accuracy) to 0.87 and 0.88 for training and testing accuracy, respectively. With performing l1 regularization, this has helped to reduce overfitting to improve future predictions.

Business Understanding of the Model

  • Improving the Recall metric is good when there are high costs associated with false negatives. The main goal was to predict the likelihood of a liability customer buying personal loans. Hence, by reducing false negatives to four (4) as shown in the above confusion matrix, this will be the desired situation. As such, missing any customer that is a potential personal loan customer will be a missed business opportunity which translates to high business losses. Hence, reducing false negatives from the initial 50 to 4 is worthwhile in this context.
  • On the other hand, having 182 false positives means the retail marketing department will lose time and money used in the marketing campaigns. That means these individuals will be contacted through campaigns, but will not convert to personal loan customers. This may not be a lost opportunity as these customers may become aware about personal loans, and when the situation change in future, they could become personal loan customers.
  • Having the Recall metric at 64% (captured in the model before evaluation) is not good for this metric as it means lost business opportunity for customers that should convert to personal loans.Hence the initial model is not selected for this reason.
  • Recall is our metric in this context and at 97% we have a better chance of ensuring we don't miss any potential customers that might be interested in a personal loan. Due to this reason,checking different parameters of LR to improves the model by reducing overfitting and increasing Recall which is the best metric for this type of business.
